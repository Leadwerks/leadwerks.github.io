Texturing Models

You can apply textures to models, or to the separate surfaces of a model.

Texturing Models

To apply a texture to a selected model, choose the texture you want from the texture category and texture lists, located in the side panel.

Press the apply texture toolbar button to paint the selection with the current texture.

Texturing Model Subobjects

To apply a texture to a single subobject of a model, first press the select subobject toolbar button to switch to subobject mode. Select the surface you wish to texture.

Press the apply texture toolbar button to paint the selected face with the current texture.

If you want to see the texture on the selected face more easily, you can press the hide mask toolbar button. This will hide the red selection mask.
