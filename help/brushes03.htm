Modifying Solids

Once a solid is created, it can be modified in several ways. Solids can be moved, scaled, rotated, skewed, and mirrored. You can also move the positions of the individual vertices.

Moving Solids

To move one or more selected solids, move the mouse inside the selection bounding box. The mouse icon will change into a four-way arrow.

Press the left mouse button, hold, and drag the mouse.

Release the left mouse button and the selected solids will be moved to the new position.

Scaling Solids

You can scale selected solids by clicking the left mouse button on any of the edge selection tabs. Drag the mouse to move the selected edge.

Rotating Solids

Solids can be rotated three ways. The first method uses the rotation mouse tool. The second method uses the rotatation dialog to rotate the selection by an arbitrary value. The third method uses the rotate toolbar buttons to perform a quick 90-degree rotation around any axis.

Click the left mouse button once within the selection bounding box of a solid. The mouse tool mode will be changed to rotation.

Click the left mouse button on any of the corner selection tabs and drag to rotate the selection.

The rotation mouse tool will lock the rotation angle to 5 degree increments. If you want to rotate the selection by an arbitrary value, select the Tools>Rotate menu item. The rotation dialog will appear. You can input the angles you wish to rotate the selection by, and press Apply to enact the rotation.

Finally, you can perform a quick 90-degree rotation around any axis by pressing the Rotate X , Rotate Y , or Rotate Z toolbar buttons.

Skewing Solids

You can perform a skew operation on solids. This is also called shearing. Click the left mouse button twice within the selection bounding box of a solid to change the mouse tool mode to skew.

Click the left mouse button on any of the edge selection tabs and drag to skew the selection.

When you release the mouse, the skew operation will be enacted, and the selection bounding box will be updated.

Mirroring Solids

Solids can be mirrored by pressing the Mirror toolbar button.
