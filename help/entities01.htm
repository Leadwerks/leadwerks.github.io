Entities

Entities are points in 3D space with a list of properties attached to them. Entities contain no mesh data, and are represented as a sprite. They are used for placing "things" in your scene, like lights, sounds, or anything else you want.

Entities are characterized by the following:

  • Entities are points in 3D space, and have no mesh data.
  • Entities contain a list of properties.
  • Entities are represented in the editor as a sprite.
