Modifying Terrain

Press the terrain editing toolbar button. This will open the terrain editing window.

The default terrain tool is sculpt. The sculpt tool can raise and lower the terrain. Move the mouse over the terrain in the 3D or XZ viewport, and click and hold the left mouse button. A hill will grow out of the terrain.

Move the mouse around while you create another hill, and a smooth mountain range will form.

Hold the control key while holding the left mouse button and valleys will form.

The flatten terrain tool will flatten the terrain to the height of the first point it is used at. If you click the left mouse button on a hill and drag the mouse, the terrain will be raised to the height at the point you first clicked.
